Procurement & Consultancy Services

Ikechi Global delivers strategic procurement and specialist consultancy services designed to reduce costs, improve supplier performance and enhance operational resilience. We combine market intelligence with process improvement to deliver measurable value.

Our procurement approach covers supplier selection, negotiation, contract management and performance monitoring. Our consultancy practice supports strategic planning, process redesign and change management to help organizations achieve sustainable improvements.

Key Offerings

  • Strategic Sourcing: Market analysis, supplier identification and competitive bidding to secure best-value suppliers.
  • Supplier Management: Performance reviews, SLAs and relationship development to ensure reliable supply.
  • Cost Optimisation: Spend analysis and process redesign to lower total cost of ownership.
  • Contracting & Compliance: Drafting, negotiating and managing contracts with compliance oversight.
  • Consultancy: Operational diagnostics, change programmes and implementation support.
